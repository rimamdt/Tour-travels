About Himalayan Dooars Tour

Embark on an enchanting journey to the Himalayan Dooars, where the rolling foothills meet the lush greenery of sprawling tea gardens, and the air hums with the melodies of nature. Nestled between the towering peaks of the Eastern Himalayas and the vibrant plains, this hidden gem offers a serene escape into unspoiled wilderness. Discover pristine rivers that wind through misty forests, encounter exotic wildlife in verdant sanctuaries, and soak in the tranquil beauty of cascading waterfalls. A tour of the Himalayan Dooars promises an unforgettable blend of adventure, culture, and natural splendor, a true paradise for the soul.
